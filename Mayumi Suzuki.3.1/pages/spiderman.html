Spider-Man

Spider-Man is one of Marvel's most iconic superheroes. His real name is Peter Parker, a bright, kind-hearted teenager from New York City.Peter Parker was an ordinary high school student until he was bitten by a genetically modified spider during a science experiment. After the bite, he discovered that he had gained incredible powers, including enhanced strength, agility, the ability to cling to walls, and a unique sense that warns him of danger, known as the Spider-Sense.At first, Peter used his powers for personal benefit. However, after his beloved Uncle Ben was tragically killed, Peter learned a life-changing lesson: "With great power comes great responsibility." Determined to honor his uncle's memory, Peter decided to dedicate his life to protecting innocent people and fighting crime as Spider-Man.Spider-Man does not possess wealth or advanced technology like some other heroes. Instead, he relies on his intelligence, quick thinking, and determination. He designed his own web-shooters, which allow him to swing through the city, capture criminals, and save people in dangerous situations.Throughout his journey, Peter struggles to balance his responsibilities as Spider-Man with his personal life, including school, friendships, family, and financial challenges. Despite these difficulties, he remains optimistic, courageous, and committed to helping others, making him one of the most relatable and beloved heroes in the Marvel Universe.

Famous Quote: "With great power comes great responsibility." – Uncle Ben
