Thor

Thor is one of Marvel's most powerful superheroes. He is the God of Thunder and the prince of Asgard, a mystical realm ruled by his father, Odin. Thor was once a proud and reckless warrior who believed that strength alone made a great leader. However, after disobeying his father and putting others in danger, he was stripped of his powers and banished to Earth to learn humility and responsibility. While living among humans, Thor discovered the true meaning of courage, compassion, and self-sacrifice. After proving himself worthy, he regained his powers and reclaimed his enchanted hammer, Mjolnir, which allows him to control thunder and lightning and grants him the ability to fly. Thor possesses incredible strength, durability, and combat skills, making him one of the strongest members of the Avengers. Despite his godlike abilities, he values friendship, loyalty, and protecting both Asgard and Earth from powerful enemies. Throughout his journey, Thor evolves from an arrogant prince into a wise and noble hero. His determination to do what is right, even in the face of great loss and hardship, has made him one of the most respected and beloved characters in the Marvel Universe.

Famous Quote: "I choose to run toward my problems, and not away from them. Because that's what heroes do." – Thor
